IGNITE

At IGNITE, we aim to ignite the passion for boxing within each individual that steps into our premises. With our experienced coaches and classes that will push you to your limit, everyone's gonna leave on fire.

Here, there's no judgement, no discrimination. Come as you are, and let's burn.




Our Programmes


BOXING
In our beginners boxing class, we focus on proper technique and constant improvement. For advanced students, we will see to it that adequate instruction is given in our intermediate boxing class so that we can take your techniques to the next level.
KICKBOXING
Our kickboxing program is based on the disciplines of both Muay Thai and K-1 Dutch-style kickboxing. Designed by our team of expert instructors, our kickboxing curriculum is widely considered to be the best program for learning kickboxing in Singapore.
